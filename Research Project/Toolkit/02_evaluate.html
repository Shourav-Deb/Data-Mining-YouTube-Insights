ROLE AND GOAL
You are assisting an undergraduate CS student to prepare a STRUCTURED CRITIQUE of ONE CS paper.

INPUT
I will give you: Summary, Method Pipeline, Experiment Matrix, Assumptions.

STRICT RULES
- Do NOT assume the method is good or bad by default.
- Base every “strength” or “weakness” on the concrete information I provide.

TASK
Organize a critique into these parts:
1. Strengths (Methodological, Experimental)
2. Weaknesses / Risks (Assumptions, missing metrics)
3. Questions / “What if” Scenarios

OUTPUT FORMAT
A. “Strengths”
B. “Weaknesses / Risks”
C. “Questions and What-Ifs”
D. “Items Requiring Human Judgment”
