AI-Assisted Research Workflow

A 10-Step "Staircase" Strategy for CS Paper Analysis

The Core Philosophy

This toolkit provides a structured "staircase" of prompts (P1–P10) to guide students from surface-level understanding to deep critique and finally to a realistic project implementation. The LLM handles structure; the student handles verification and judgment.

P6

Assumption & Limitation Finder

Crucial for critique. Distinguishes between what is stated explicitly and what is inferred by the method's design.

  • Explicit: Stated constraints (e.g., "Assumes normally distributed noise").
  • Inferred: Implied by design (e.g., "Requires large memory due to O(N^2) attention").
  • Limitations: Open problems and self-admitted weak points.
