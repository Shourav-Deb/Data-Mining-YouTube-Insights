PAPER CONTEXT
Title: [Insert Title]
Authors: <optional>
I will paste the ABSTRACT and INTRODUCTION of the paper after this message.

STRICT RULES
- Do NOT invent facts that are not clearly stated in the text I provide.

- Use simple, direct English. Avoid flowery language.

TASKS

2. Produce:

FINAL FORMAT
Use headings: “Plain-English Summary”, “Key Points”, “Unclear Points”.

P2 – Component Extractor

👨‍🏫 Teacher's Goal
Get the mental model skeleton: problem, formal setup, method modules, experiments, contributions. 
ROLE AND GOAL
You are assisting an undergraduate CS student to dissect the structure of ONE CS research paper.
I will paste the TABLE OF CONTENTS or SECTION HEADINGS and possibly some snippets.

STRICT RULES
- Do NOT guess content that is not supported by the headings/snippets.

TASK
Identify and describe the main conceptual components (P0: Problem, P1: Setup, P2..: Methods, EXP: Experiments).
For each component: Give it a short ID and Label, and write 2–3 sentences.

OUTPUT FORMAT
1) List of Components
   - ID: <ID>
